A walk in nauture walks the soul back home

"Every country is beautiful in its own way."

Ecotourism team

Every country is adorned with its own unique tapestry of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical marvels waiting to be explored. From the lush rainforests of Costa Rica to the snow-capped peaks of the Swiss Alps, each nation offers a myriad of breathtaking landscapes to enchant and inspire travelers. Ecotourism has emerged as a powerful force in many countries, encouraging visitors to appreciate and preserve the delicate balance of nature. Whether it's the pristine beaches of the Seychelles or the majestic fjords of Norway, ecotourism destinations provide an opportunity for travelers to connect with the environment in a sustainable and responsible manner. Cultural diversity adds another layer of allure to countries worldwide. From the ancient temples of Angkor Wat in Cambodia to the vibrant markets of Marrakech in Morocco, every corner of the globe is infused with its own distinct heritage and traditions, inviting travelers to immerse themselves in the richness of different cultures.

In addition to natural wonders and cultural treasures, the warmth and hospitality of local communities further enhance the beauty of countries around the world. Whether it's savoring homemade cuisine with a local family in Italy or participating in a traditional dance ceremony in Bali, the kindness and generosity of people worldwide leave an indelible mark on travelers' hearts.

Some beauties of Moldova

Orheiul Vechi

Orheiul Vechi is a historical and archaeological complex located along the Răut River, known for its stunning natural landscapes and cultural heritage. The site features ancient cave monasteries, carved into limestone cliffs, offering panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.

Codrii Forest

Codrii Forest, also known as Codru Forest, is one of the largest remaining forested areas in Moldova, covering vast expanses of rolling hills and dense woodlands. The forest is home to diverse flora and fauna, including oak, beech, and pine trees, as well as rare species such as the European bison and the golden eagle

Tipova Monastery

Tipova Monastery is a historic monastery complex located in a picturesque gorge along the Dniester River, surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and lush greenery. The monastery dates back to the 10th century and is renowned for its stunning cave churches and frescoes.

Nistru River Canyon

The Nistru River Canyon, also known as the Dniester Canyon, is a natural wonder located in northeastern Moldova, offering spectacular views of rugged cliffs and meandering river bends. The canyon is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with opportunities for hiking, rock climbing, and river rafting

Saharna Monastery

Saharna Monastery is a serene religious complex nestled in the picturesque Saharna Valley, surrounded by lush forests and cascading waterfalls. The monastery is renowned for its beautiful setting and spiritual significance, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike

Vădenița

Vădenița Monastery is a hidden gem located in the northern part of Moldova, nestled amidst rolling hills and pristine countryside. The monastery features picturesque wooden churches and chapels, adorned with intricate carvings and colorful frescoes.

Cricova

Cricova Wine Cellars are a unique underground wine cellar complex located near the town of Cricova, renowned for their vast underground tunnels and extensive wine collections. The cellars, carved into limestone rock, extend for kilometers underground and provide the perfect conditions for aging wine.

Padurea Domneasca

Padurea Domneasca Biosphere Reserve is a protected area located in northern Moldova, encompassing diverse ecosystems such as forests, wetlands, and meadows. The reserve is home to a wide range of plant and animal species, including rare and endangered species such as the European bison and the white-tailed eagle
