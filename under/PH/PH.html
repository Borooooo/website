GO NATURAL

A walk in nauture walks the soul back home

"Every country is beautiful in its own way."

Ecotourism team

Every country is adorned with its own unique tapestry of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical marvels waiting to be explored. From the lush rainforests of Costa Rica to the snow-capped peaks of the Swiss Alps, each nation offers a myriad of breathtaking landscapes to enchant and inspire travelers. Ecotourism has emerged as a powerful force in many countries, encouraging visitors to appreciate and preserve the delicate balance of nature. Whether it's the pristine beaches of the Seychelles or the majestic fjords of Norway, ecotourism destinations provide an opportunity for travelers to connect with the environment in a sustainable and responsible manner. Cultural diversity adds another layer of allure to countries worldwide. From the ancient temples of Angkor Wat in Cambodia to the vibrant markets of Marrakech in Morocco, every corner of the globe is infused with its own distinct heritage and traditions, inviting travelers to immerse themselves in the richness of different cultures.

In addition to natural wonders and cultural treasures, the warmth and hospitality of local communities further enhance the beauty of countries around the world. Whether it's savoring homemade cuisine with a local family in Italy or participating in a traditional dance ceremony in Bali, the kindness and generosity of people worldwide leave an indelible mark on travelers' hearts.

Some beauties of Philippines

Palawan

Palawan is an archipelago province located in the western part of the Philippines, known for its stunning natural beauty and pristine beaches. Highlights include El Nido, famous for its towering limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and hidden lagoons; Coron, home to stunning shipwrecks and vibrant coral reefs perfect for diving and snorkeling; and the Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature, showcasing a mesmerizing subterranean river system.

Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills are a geological formation located in the Bohol province of the Philippines, consisting of thousands of conical hills covered in green grass that turns brown during the dry season, resembling giant chocolate kisses. These iconic hills are a popular tourist destination, offering panoramic views from observation points such as the Chocolate Hills Complex in Carmen.

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are ancient agricultural terraces carved into the mountains of Ifugao province in northern Luzon, often referred to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World." These stunning terraces, built over 2,000 years ago by indigenous tribes, showcase intricate engineering and farming techniques that have sustained generations of rice farmers

Siargao Island

Siargao Island is a paradise for surfers located in the province of Surigao del Norte, known for its world-class waves, pristine beaches, and laid-back island vibe. The island is home to famous surf breaks such as Cloud 9, where surfers from around the globe gather to ride the legendary barrels. Beyond surfing, visitors can explore the island's natural beauty, including secluded lagoons, mangrove forests, and vibrant coral reefs perfect for snorkeling and diving.

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano is an active stratovolcano located in the province of Albay in southeastern Luzon, renowned for its perfectly symmetrical cone shape and stunning landscapes. The volcano is a popular hiking destination, with trails leading to the crater offering breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside and the Bicol region

Tubbataha Reefs

Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in the Sulu Sea, known for its pristine coral reefs, diverse marine life, and crystal-clear waters. The park is a haven for scuba divers, offering opportunities to explore vibrant coral gardens, encounter large pelagic species such as sharks and manta rays, and marvel at the sheer abundance of marine biodiversity

Hinatuan Enchanted

Hinatuan Enchanted River is a mystical river located in the province of Surigao del Sur on the island of Mindanao, known for its mesmerizing blue waters and enchanting natural beauty. The river is surrounded by lush greenery and limestone cliffs, creating a magical atmosphere that attracts visitors from far and wide.

Batanes

The Batanes Islands are a group of idyllic islands located in the northernmost part of the Philippines, known for their rugged landscapes, traditional Ivatan culture, and unspoiled beauty. The islands are characterized by rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, and picturesque stone houses, offering stunning vistas at every turn
