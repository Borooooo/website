GO NATURAL

A walk in nauture walks the soul back home

"Every country is beautiful in its own way."

Ecotourism team

Every country is adorned with its own unique tapestry of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical marvels waiting to be explored. From the lush rainforests of Costa Rica to the snow-capped peaks of the Swiss Alps, each nation offers a myriad of breathtaking landscapes to enchant and inspire travelers. Ecotourism has emerged as a powerful force in many countries, encouraging visitors to appreciate and preserve the delicate balance of nature. Whether it's the pristine beaches of the Seychelles or the majestic fjords of Norway, ecotourism destinations provide an opportunity for travelers to connect with the environment in a sustainable and responsible manner. Cultural diversity adds another layer of allure to countries worldwide. From the ancient temples of Angkor Wat in Cambodia to the vibrant markets of Marrakech in Morocco, every corner of the globe is infused with its own distinct heritage and traditions, inviting travelers to immerse themselves in the richness of different cultures.

In addition to natural wonders and cultural treasures, the warmth and hospitality of local communities further enhance the beauty of countries around the world. Whether it's savoring homemade cuisine with a local family in Italy or participating in a traditional dance ceremony in Bali, the kindness and generosity of people worldwide leave an indelible mark on travelers' hearts.

Some beauties of Kosovo

Rugova Gorge

Rugova Gorge is a breathtaking natural wonder located in the western part of Kosovo. Carved by the Rugova River, this stunning canyon is characterized by towering limestone cliffs, lush forests, and crystal-clear streams. Visitors can hike along scenic trails, rock climb on the gorge's rugged cliffs, and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Mirusha Waterfalls

Mirusha Waterfalls are a series of cascading waterfalls nestled in the scenic countryside of central Kosovo. Surrounded by dense forests and rocky terrain, these picturesque waterfalls offer a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life. Visitors can swim in the natural pools, picnic in the serene surroundings, and capture stunning photographs of the cascading waters

harri National Park

Sharri National Park is a pristine wilderness area located in southern Kosovo, near the border with North Macedonia and Albania. Spanning rugged mountains, verdant forests, and alpine meadows, the park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Visitors can hike through the park's diverse landscapes, spot native wildlife such as brown bears and lynxes, and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks.

Germia Park

Germia Park is a popular recreational area located near the capital city of Pristina. This expansive park features lush greenery, meandering trails, and tranquil lakes, making it an ideal spot for picnics, hiking, and outdoor activities. Visitors can relax in shaded groves, rent bicycles to explore the park's scenic pathways, and enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline.

White Drin Waterfall

White Drin Waterfall is a stunning natural attraction located in the western part of Kosovo, near the town of Peja. Fed by the White Drin River, this picturesque waterfall plunges over rocky cliffs into a pristine pool below. Visitors can hike along scenic trails to reach the waterfall, swim in the refreshing waters, and admire the surrounding scenery

Janjevo Lake

Janjevo Lake is a tranquil reservoir nestled amid rolling hills and lush forests in central Kosovo. Offering serene surroundings and pristine waters, the lake is a popular spot for fishing, boating, and picnicking. Visitors can relax on the shores, take leisurely boat rides, and enjoy the peaceful ambiance of this idyllic natural retreat.

Bear Sanctuary Prishtina

Bear Sanctuary Prishtina is a unique conservation area located in the outskirts of Pristina. Home to rescued brown bears, the sanctuary provides a safe and natural habitat for these majestic creatures. Visitors can observe the bears from viewing platforms, learn about their rehabilitation process, and support conservation efforts through educational programs and donations.

Brezovica Ski Resort

Brezovica Ski Resort is a winter paradise nestled in the Šar Mountains of southern Kosovo. Offering pristine slopes, stunning mountain scenery, and excellent skiing conditions, the resort attracts winter sports enthusiasts from near and far. Visitors can enjoy downhill skiing, snowboarding, and other snow-related activities against the backdrop of breathtaking alpine landscapes.
